Recognize the Symptoms

While snakes are mesmerizing creatures, they can be hazardous, so staying informed and cautious is essential. Sometimes, a snake may bite, and in such cases, recognizing symptoms quickly is crucial for taking prompt action when every moment counts.

Bleeding
Breathing difficulties
Sweating
Vomiting
Blurred Vision
